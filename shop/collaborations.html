Our Process

The Collaboration Process

Our collaborations are carefully crafted partnerships with the world's most respected breeders, combining our authenticated genetics with their exceptional strains to create unique varieties.

Partner Selection

We carefully select partners based on their reputation, breeding expertise, and commitment to genetic preservation. Each collaboration begins with a shared vision for creating exceptional cannabis varieties.

Genetic Development

Our authenticated strain genetics are carefully crossed with partner varieties through a meticulous breeding process. Multiple generations are tested to ensure stability and desired characteristics.

Quality Assurance

Each collaboration undergoes rigorous testing for potency, terpene profile, growth characteristics, and genetic stability. Only varieties meeting our strict standards are released.
