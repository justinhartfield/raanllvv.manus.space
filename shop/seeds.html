Seed Options

Choose Your Perfect Seeds

All our strains are available in different seed types to suit your specific needs and growing preferences.

Feminized Seeds

Specially bred to produce only female plants, eliminating the need to identify and remove male plants. Ideal for most growers seeking maximum yield of flowering plants.

Autoflowering Seeds

These seeds automatically transition from vegetative growth to flowering based on age rather than light cycle changes. Perfect for beginners or those seeking a faster harvest.

Regular Seeds

Natural, non-modified seeds that produce both male and female plants. Ideal for breeders, seed production, or those seeking to preserve the original genetics of legendary strains.
